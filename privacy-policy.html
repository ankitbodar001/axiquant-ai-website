Privacy Policy

How AxiQuant AI handles the information you share with us.

Last updated: 30 June 2026

AxiQuant AI ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use axiquantai.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you use our contact form or email us, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email address, company, and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, our hosting provider records basic technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. We use this to keep the site secure and to understand how it is used.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to respond to your enquiries, to provide and improve our services, and to maintain the security of our site. We do not sell your personal data.

Service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run our site and communications, including our hosting provider (Netlify) and our contact-form delivery service (EmailJS). These providers process data on our behalf and only as needed to deliver their service.

Cookies and local storage

Our site uses minimal local storage needed for functionality — for example, remembering your light or dark theme preference. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites.

Data retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as necessary to respond to you and to maintain reasonable business records, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

International transfers

We are based in India and may process information there. Where we handle information relating to individuals in the EU or UK, we take steps to protect it consistent with applicable data-protection laws.

Your rights

Depending on where you live — including under the GDPR for individuals in the EU and UK — you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to how we process it. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@axiquantai.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page, with the date it was last updated shown above.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email info@axiquantai.com or write to us at AxiQuant AI, Surat, Gujarat, India.
