Manufacturing

Your factory generates terabytes of sensor data every day, yet unplanned downtime, quality escapes, and energy waste still cost you. The problem usually isn't the data — it's that the data was never connected to intelligence.

How we help

  • Predict equipment failures days to weeks ahead to reduce unplanned downtime
  • Optimize production scheduling against real demand signals, not last month's spreadsheet
  • Detect quality defects at the source, before a batch is scrapped
  • Surface energy and consumption anomalies across shifts and assets

Insurance

Reporting built on manually maintained spreadsheets is slow, error-prone, and impossible to fully trust — exactly when accuracy matters most.

  • Add self-service querying so business users get answers without waiting
