Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to what clients ask before working with us. Don't see yours? Get in touch.

Working with us

How do we get started?

It starts with a free, no-obligation consultation. You tell us about your data and the decisions you're trying to improve, and we tell you honestly whether and how we can help. If it's a fit, we scope a focused first project with clear deliverables, a timeline, and a fixed price before any work begins.

Do you work with companies outside India?

Yes — most of our focus is on clients in the US, UK, Europe, and the UAE. We keep working-hours overlap with the UK, EU, and Middle East, and run structured asynchronous updates for US clients, so distance and time zones never slow things down.

You're a small team — can you handle our project?

Being small is the reason the work is better, not a limitation. There's no junior bench: the people who scope your project are the people who build it. You get senior attention, direct access, and fast decisions, without the overhead and hand-offs of a large agency. For work beyond our capacity, we're upfront about it rather than overcommitting.

Do you work with startups and smaller companies?

Yes. We work with organisations of all sizes, from early-stage startups to established enterprises. What matters is that you have data and decisions worth improving.

Pricing & engagement

How do you price projects?

We scope a fixed-price first project so you know the cost upfront, with no surprises. Larger or ongoing engagements can be milestone-based or run on a retainer. We work within your budget and your existing stack rather than forcing expensive rip-and-replace.

What's a typical project timeline?

It depends on scope. Smaller pieces of work can be delivered in a few weeks; full implementations typically range from two to six months. We give you a clear estimate after the initial consultation, and we work in short, visible increments so you see progress throughout.

Do you provide ongoing support after delivery?

Yes. We stay available to maintain, extend, and improve what we've built as your needs grow. Support can be arranged as needed or on a retainer.

Security & ownership

How do you handle our data and security?

We work inside your environment and access controls, follow least-privilege practices, and stay mindful of GDPR and data-protection obligations for EU and UK data. We're happy to sign an NDA before you share anything sensitive, and to follow your organisation's security requirements.

Will we own what you build?

Yes. We build within your stack, and you own the code, the dashboards, and the intellectual property. There's no lock-in and no ongoing dependency on us beyond the support you choose.

Capabilities

What services and technologies do you work with?

We cover AI/ML, data engineering, data analytics, generative AI, data visualisation, and agentic AI. We work hands-on with tools like Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, Azure Data Factory, Databricks, SQL, Spark, Python, and modern ML and deep-learning frameworks — and we adapt to whatever stack you already use.
